B-Quiz

"What's your Biz Quotient? Enhance it by participating in the Quiz comprising the world of Business, Industry & Economy"

Rules:

  • 1.Each team will consist of 2-4 members.
  • 2.The quiz will be of 3 rounds, an "Elimination Round", a "Visual Round" and a "Rapid Fire Round".
  • 3.The rules for each round will be declared at the beginning of the round.
  • 4.The top 5 teams from the first round will qualify for the subsequent rounds.
  • 5. If none of the teams are able to answer a question in the latter rounds, the audience will be given a chance to answer.
  • 6. If teams are tied at the end of the quiz, an on-the-spot "Tie-Breaker Round" will take place to break the tie.
  • 7.If there are no ties, the team with the maximum number of points will win.
